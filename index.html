Innovation camp

The concept of an Innovation Camp is to bring people from different fields together to define, develop and generate new ideas and consepts that can change business models, solve global challenges and develop a local culture of innovativness.

Our Mission

Inova loves ideas. If we could borrow Gyro Gearloose's thinking cap, we would, but we believe the next best thing is to bring creative people together. If two heads think better than one, imagine what ten can do, or twenty, or even fifty. We think you get the point. Inova brings people together to solve the problems of tomorrow.
