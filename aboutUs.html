About Inova

Inova is a newly founded company, who is exited to bring innovation camps to the masses. Our business is focused around frugal innovation, and wanting to make it accessible to any business. We also want to make it easy for people who are interested in participating in innovation camps to do so. We are happy to share our experience with you, and should you be interested please feel free to contact us.
